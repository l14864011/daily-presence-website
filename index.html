How it works

Three steps to now

Personalized to your moment, grounded in the present — just you and right now.

✍️

Name your moment

Type what you're doing — cooking, walking, feeding your baby. Optionally add a photo to personalize your guidance.

🎧

Receive audio guidance

A personalized, spoken guidance tuned to your exact moment — grounded in sensory awareness, not judgment.

🍃

Let it pass

Your input is not stored after your guidance is created. Like the moment itself — it comes, and it goes.

Our philosophy

"Moments are meant to pass."

Your text and photos are sent securely to generate your guidance — and not stored after it's created. Presence is built around the belief that some things are meant only for right now.
