Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

Presence ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Xiuling Liu. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

1. Information You May Provide

You may choose to provide input such as text or an image when using the App. You are not required to provide any input to use the App.

This input is used only to generate your mindfulness guidance and is not stored after processing.

2. How Your Information Is Used

To generate your experience:

  • Any input you choose to provide (such as text or an image) is securely sent to OpenAI to generate personalized mindfulness guidance
  • The generated text may be sent to Inworld to produce audio playback

Important:

  • OpenAI receives user-provided input
  • Inworld receives only generated text, not raw user input

3. Data Storage

We do not store your input (text or images) or the generated guidance after your session is complete.

We may store limited account-related and usage information, including:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • User preferences (such as voice or settings)
  • Session information (such as session length or usage timing)

This information is used only to operate and improve the App.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the App:

  • OpenAI — processes user input to generate guidance
  • Inworld — converts generated text into audio
  • Firebase (Google) — used for authentication and limited data storage (such as account information, preferences, and session metadata)

These providers process data only as needed to deliver the App's features.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data for advertising or profiling purposes.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information during transmission.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Choose whether to provide input
  • Stop using the App at any time
  • Request account deletion if applicable

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
